# Code of Conduct

## Expected behavior

Contributors should communicate clearly, assume good intent, and keep review feedback focused on code, safety, documentation, and maintainability.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, personal attacks, credential sharing, unsafe trading instructions, and attempts to bypass project safety controls are not acceptable.

## Safety-first collaboration

QuantGod contains trading research and MT5 integration code. Discussions and changes must preserve the project's safety boundaries: advisory AI, readonly evidence planes, push-only notifications, manual authorization, and no hidden live trading escalation.

## Enforcement

Repository maintainers may remove content, reject contributions, or block participants that violate this policy or create safety risks.
